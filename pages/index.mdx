# The People's Network

We install Helium LongFi Hotspots to provide wireless network coverage for IoT (Internet of Things) devices. IoT devices include smart phones, smart homes, smart refrigerators, smartwatches, smart fire alarms, smart door locks, smart bicycles, medical sensors, fitness trackers, and much more. There are currently over 30 billion IoT devices in the world, and the number continues to grow.
